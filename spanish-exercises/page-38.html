SONIDOS Y LETRAS

In Spanish and in English there are 5 vowel letters: a, e, i, o and u. However, in English they can have different pronunciations (as the o in go, do, got), whereas in Spanish each one of these vowel letters has only one possible pronunciation. Vowels in Spanish are pronounced as single short sounds:

  • a as in alpha
  • e as in elephant
  • i as in India
  • o as in cost
  • u as in do

Make sure you do not pronounce a single vowel letter with two vowel sounds.
