PHRASES INCLUDING IR AND SALIR

There are certain collocations that contain the verbs ir and salir. It is useful to learn each expression as a whole (as a chunk):

  • Ir al cine / ir al teatro
  • Ir de compras / ir de excursión
  • Salir de noche / salir de fiesta

Remember that ir de compras (to go shopping) is not the same as ir a comprar (to go and buy) or hacer la compra (to do the shopping).
