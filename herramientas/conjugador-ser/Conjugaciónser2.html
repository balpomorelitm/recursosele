The Verb "Ser"

The verb ser is fundamental in Spanish. It's used to talk about permanent or long-lasting attributes. A helpful way to remember its uses is with the acronym DOCTOR:

  • Description: For essential qualities. (e.g., "El cielo es azul.")
  • Occupation: For jobs or professions. (e.g., "Mi madre es doctora.")
  • Characteristic: For personality traits. (e.g., "Tú eres muy amable.")
  • Time: For days, dates, and hours. (e.g., "Hoy es martes.")
  • Origin: Where someone or something is from. (e.g., "Nosotros somos de Canadá.")
  • Relationship: For family ties and friendships. (e.g., "Ellos son mis hermanos.")
